Frequently Asked Questions

How long do I have to answer each question?
You have 2 minutes per question. The timer is displayed in the top-right corner and will turn red when you have 30 seconds left.
Can I pause a quiz once I've started?
No, quizzes cannot be paused once started. However, if time runs out, the system will automatically submit your current selection.
How is my score calculated?
Your score is calculated as a percentage: (Correct Answers / Total Questions) × 100. Detailed analytics are available in your progress section.
What are the keyboard shortcuts?
Use A, B, C, D keys to select answers quickly, and press Enter to submit. Numbers 1, 2, 3, 4 also work for option selection.
How do I track my progress?
Visit the Progress page to see detailed analytics, including topic-wise performance, score trends, and achievement tracking.
